Distribution

Information related to the distributions installed on the systems that popcorn submissions are received from.

Show a distribution

Information about a distribution, including all the submissions received for that distribution.

GET /distro/<distro_name>/<distro_version>

Arguments:

distro_name
Canonical distribution name (e.g. "openSUSE", "SUSE Linux Enterprise Server")
distro_version
Distribution version (e.g. 12.1, 11)

Submissions for each distribution version
